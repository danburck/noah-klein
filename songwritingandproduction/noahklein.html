Songwriting & Production

Noah Klein

Noah Klein is my main project. I have been working on it since 2017. Through this project I have professionalized my skills as a songwriter, producer and mixer.

In 2020 I released my second ep "Reflections". I wrote all six songs on the EP over two-months on Ableton using a Juno 60, Telecaster guitar, Korg piano, and Schoeps microphones. It was my first time collaborating with rappers including Mike Evans (USA) on "Hyper Real Love" and Souly (GER) for "Cassettes"

"Lights Off" was my second single in 2020. For this production I experimented with new vocal patterns and tried out a different mixing technique to create warm dynamics.

"Hasting" was my first single released in 2020. I focussed on simplifying arrangements to develop an easily listenable song with a more upbeat melody.

"Seasons" was the first ep I worked on. I released it in 2017. It got me into using analog synths and perform live at festivals and clubs around Germany.
